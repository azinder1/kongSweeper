Instructions:


The goal of Kongsweeper is to uncover all the squares that don't contain TNT without being blown up.

Select your difficulty, enter your name and add the width of your grid (between 5 and 30 squares).

Left click to reveal a square.

Right click to flag a possible bomb.

The counter will tell you how many flags remain.

Clear all the bombs in the fastest time and earn your spot on the leaderboard!
